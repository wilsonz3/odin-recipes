Sushi Recipe

Ingredients

  • Raw Sashimi
  • Cheese
  • Avocado
  • Sushi Rice
  • Seaweed
  • Bamboo roller

Steps

  1. Spread seaweed with a layer of prepared sushi rice
  2. cut up salmon and other ingredients into small strips
  3. place them onto bamboo mat and roll it up tightly
